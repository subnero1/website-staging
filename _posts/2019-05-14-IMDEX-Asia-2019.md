Subnero exhibited at IMDEX Asia 2019, held from 14–16 May at the Changi Exhibition Centre in Singapore. Over the first two days, the Subnero booth attracted visitors from a wide range of countries, with strong interest in our technology and upcoming projects. The exhibition concluded with Subnero winning the “What’s Next” pitching session, marking a strong close to the event.
